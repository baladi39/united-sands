## Homepage

### Part 1: Hero / Full-Screen Reveal

- Appears after the logo intro zoom-in animation
- **Logo** in the left corner
- **Menu** button in the right corner
- **"Request Project"** CTA button for fast call to action
- **Language toggle** (EN / AR)
- Main banner visual is related to the **arch in the logo** (cosmic/golden arch with particles)
- Main slogan: **"WE SHAPE THE FUTURE"** centered
- **Scroll-down indicator** icon to guide users
- Banner text: intro text + **"Our Story"** button linking to the About Us inner page

### Part 2: Scroll-Driven Arch Zoom Animation

- As the user scrolls, the background **zooms in** to show "walking inside the frame/arch"
- Creates an immersive, divine feel as users move deeper into the site
- **Reference:** [promo.emotion-agency.com](https://promo.emotion-agency.com/)
- Reina attached a video showing the desired effect

### Part 3: Scroll Text Animation (Between Sections)

- Fully animated based on scroll — large text/sentence **slides as the user scrolls**
- Text is made smaller so the whole message stays visible while sliding
- **Reference:** [relm.co](https://www.relm.co/)
- A **gradient circle** (core branding element) follows the user through the next few sections
- The circle keeps visual identity consistent and adds a smooth, immersive feel
- **Reference for circle:** also on [relm.co](https://www.relm.co/)

### Part 4: "Why Choose United Sands?" Stats Section

- Displays stats like **"12 Cities"** and industrial zones
- Areas: scale of reach, customer success, experience, team strength, strategic experience
- **Next/previous click buttons** to navigate (not scroll-triggered)
- **Reference:** [julianfella.de](https://www.julianfella.de/) (the second section)

### Part 5: "Serving You Across Every Step"

- Clean layout design with photos
- Photos have a **dim color overlay** to keep it sleek
- Button that takes the user to the inner page for more details
- **NO ANIMATION** on this section

### Part 6: Sectoral Evolution / Sectors Section

- Title: **"Sectoral Evolution Through Technology"**
- 6 sector windows: Smart Cities, Environment/ESG, Industrial Manufacturing, Utilities/Energy, Transport/Mobility, Satellite Imagery
- As the user scrolls, the 6 windows **move up faster** than the middle text (parallax)
- Windows eventually **gather and align in the middle** of the screen
- Creates a parallax feel where sectors become the main focus

### Part 7: Integrated Digital Services

- 3 services: **Digital Twin Solutions, Smart City Solutions, GIS & Geospatial Intelligence**
- Simple **click-over** interaction for each sector
- No complex animation — designed to be easily expandable for future services
- A **"Request Project"** button visible below

### Part 8: "Request Project" Button Morph

- The "Request Project" button from the corner **extends and morphs** into a large circle in the middle of the screen on scroll
- Creates a smooth, organic transition pulling attention to the CTA
- **Reference:** [scrgroup.com](https://www.scrgroup.com/) (the "Let's Talk" button section)

### Part 9: Portfolio Section

- Simple — **3 newest projects** displayed on the homepage
- **Next/previous** navigation to click through them
- The **gradient circle** appears from the right as you scroll and settles into position
- Continues the "path" visual from the previous section

### Part 10: "Why Being Saudi-Born Matters"

### Part 11: Team Section

- **"THE TEAM"** — team member cards with photos
- Each card shows: name, **"Read Bio"** button, **LinkedIn icon** linking to their profiles

### Part 12: "Now Meet The Strategy"

### Part 13: Partners & Contact (Circle Morph Finale)
